# Fictional Paper & DGE Sessions

**[日本語版](./INDEX.md)**

> **All documents in this collection are entirely fictional.**
>
> A fictional paper "From Grammar to IDE" was written, then turned into a peer review drama using the DGE (Design Gap Exploration) methodology.
>
> Three fictional reviewers:
> - **R1** — A category theory purist and theorist
> - **R2** — A benchmark-obsessed pragmatist
> - **R3** — A functional programming zealot who worships Haskell as gospel
>
> Along with the hot-tempered **Senpai** (author) and peacemaker **Kōhai**, they expose design gaps through the review process.
>
> Enjoy this academic peer review entertainment — full of laughs, tears, and furious rebuttals.
